About Us

Our Story, Mission & Vision

Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Thiru. V. Ramamoorthy with a humble vision of providing quality primary education to children of Virudhunagar and nearby villages, Venkateshwara Vidyalaya has grown from a small nursery class of 25 students into a full-fledged primary institution serving over 850 students.

Located at No. 12, Madurai Road, Virudhunagar – 626 001, the school serves families across Virudhunagar, Srivilliputhur, Rajapalayam, and surrounding areas with dedicated bus routes.

Our Mission

To nurture every young mind with quality education, strong moral values, and cultural pride – preparing children to be responsible citizens rooted in Tamil heritage and ready for the modern world.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted nursery and primary school in the Virudhunagar district, where every child – regardless of background – receives the foundation they deserve.

Admission Process

1
Collect Application

Visit school office or download form. No application fee for SC/ST families.

2
Submit Documents

Birth certificate, transfer certificate (if applicable), Aadhaar card, passport photos & caste certificate.

3
Simple Assessment

For Std I–V: a basic readiness check. Nursery, LKG, UKG are direct admissions.

4
Parent Interaction

Brief meeting with the Headmistress to understand the child's needs and school routines.

5
Confirmation

Pay admission fee, collect school kit, uniform details and bus route information.
